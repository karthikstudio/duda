About

Who Am I

My name is Duda Quaresma, and I am a mother of four girls based in East London. I am a speaker and transformational life coach. I moved from Portugal to the UK, which came with many challenges.

Each day was a struggle but through hard work, determination and self-belief I can stand tall and be proud of how far I have come. I am a role model for my children, as their Mother I want to inspire them and other women that they can do it too.

What I Do

My passion lies working with women, in particular those who are mothers. I have worked with many top coaches and want to impact others with my knowledge and experience.

  • I frequently hold events in London
  • Private 1-2-1 coaching and group coaching sessions
  • I am a speaker for personal development events
  • I hold small practical workshops in London

My Story

Where I come from?

I moved from Portugal to the UK and left my mother and two sisters behind. I came to start a new life here with my partner and we were faced with many challenges. My partner was living in Newcastle and I stayed at my niece’s house for almost one year with no income. I became pregnant again so I was offered a hostel to stay at until a permanent place became available, trying to survive on twenty pounds a week.

My first opportunity

My 3-year-old daughter was crying every day, saying she wanted to go back to her grandmother and aunties, and for a mother this was a torture. After seven months in the hostel we were offered a place to live. From there our life started to improve, with my partner moving from Newcastle to be with us. I started to work at hotel as a cleaner and within a year became head housekeeper.

Parents, Inspiration

My big inspiration is my mother, for what she has been through in this life. Since her teenage years, she had so many challenges but never gave up. She has a huge faith, is powerful and is such a positive person despite losing one of her children at childbirth which was potentially fatal to her. My father was a role model, ambassador, and such a great leader.

Following My Dreams

It is not easy for mothers that work, especially when you have four children aged from three to eighteen. I gave up my part time cleaning in order to follow my dreams. I now attend university, I love cooking and travelling with my children. I also have my own moments – I love dancing. My energy comes from the constant support from my husband.

A Mother's Gift

Like many parents our children become our world, I want to be a role model for my four princesses and encourage them to pursue their passions. However if I want to teach them this I must walk the talk and show them that they should live how they want to live and pursue what makes them happy. My four daughters fuel my passion and drive, and I want to be an example to all that if a mother who was a cleaner can do it, then anyone can do it.

The Power of Mindset

Sometimes when I was at the hotel cleaning and a guest was rude to me, I never got upset. I knew that my future was going to be amazing, and this moment was just a chapter of my life. Since I was a young girl I knew that I was born for a reason, that my name will be called to a higher place. I never have been scared of hard work, as I trust my instinct and know my time will come. I believe and I make it happen.

Face Your Fear

I truly believe that your current situation doesn’t define you. When you have a dream inside you and you just follow your heart, your actions will get you to where you want to be. I started attending events and worked with top level coaches creating a high net worth of connections.I never have been afraid of networking and presenting myself to others. I share my dreams and passions confidently.

My Life Purpose

I speak from my heart to other mothers and love to motivate them. I make them believe that no matter what’s going on in life they should never give up on their dreams. I want to travel all over the world and make my voice heard. I want to show people by living by example, that you really can live the life you want. The only person that can give you what you want is you, it all starts with you, and I want to work with people helping them live their dreams.
